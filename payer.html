Payer

Seamless bill paying application

Introduction

Paying of bill is one of the everyday activities humans do, you can’t do a day without paying one bill or the other, there will always be a bill to pay for.

Payer is an E-wallet mobile application used for paying bills, transfering and withdrawing money without stresss. With this app, you can automate your regular bill payment i.e you don’t have to manually pay your bill, what you just have to do is to programme your monthly payment or yearly payment and the app makes payment from your wallet.

This project was planned and executed by myself. As a UI/UX Designer, my role was to conduct user experience (UX) research and create a clean and simple clickable mobile app.

Challenge

Research into the problem

There are a lot of E-wallet app on playstore which are really nice, but they all do not have the feature of automatically paying some fixed bill i.e paying of electricty bill, internet subscription, house rent e.t.c.

Solution

I came up with a full fledge mobile application, with this app you can pay your bills, store banking card, easily track your expenses and also automatically pay some bill in the future.

User Flow

Wireframes
